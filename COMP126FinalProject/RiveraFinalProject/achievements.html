Past UNC Men's Basketball Achievements

As fans of UNC Men's Basketball, many may turn to achievements as a way of comparing our team's past to the teams we are up against. In the previous NCAA season, the basketball team made it as a National Runner-up while defeating Duke on their head coach, Mike Krzyzewski's, last game. All of the following numerical data and corresponding team information is taken from the source: Wikipedia.com.

Titles, Wins, and Ranks

In previous years, the team has garnered up an impressive amount of rankings, titles, and wins that have added to a multitude of other achievements under their belt. Currently, the team stand at 2,321 All-time wins. This translates into an almost 74% All-time win rate. 49 players have been chosen 78 times for All-Americans honors. In regards to titles, over the years the team has gathered 31 ACC regular season titles, with 18 additional ACC Tournament titles

NCAA

When it comes to the NCAA, the team has never fell short in continuing to deliver great games and bring home notable achievements. Solely within the NCAA Tournaments, they have garnered 131 wins, attending the Final Four 21 times. In the past, they have wont NCAA championships 6 times, becoming runner-ups an additional 6 times. Within the tournament, they total a number of 52 appearances, impressively defeating the No.1 ranked team in the country a 14 times.
